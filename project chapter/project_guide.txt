Aim and Objectives of the study
Usually your aim is tied to your topic which should reflect your goalObjectives is the roadmap to getting to the goal. It should be minimum four.  The objectives must be implementable using terms such as Design, Develop, Implement and Evaluate. However, this solely depend on the structural tendency of the research layout. 
Scope of the study
The scope of a study refers to the extent or range of the research area that will  be investigated. It defines what will be covered and what will not be covered in the study. 
Significance of the study
Research Projects are designed to solve  problem. The important of such project to the sector is what is classified as the significance   
Limitations of the study
In the course of any research work there are bound to be some challenges. These challenges could be pre-planed, above your control. However, they are expected to be listed and explained
